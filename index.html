Our Story

How we met:

Summer 2012 Bloomington IN - Matt and his friend are hungry and go to Scottys Brewhouse. Their cute hostess Megan knew Matt's friend since elementry school. The friend introduces the two. Little did either party know they had just met their future spouse. In the Fall of 2013 they meet again at bar called Kilroys. Both seniors at Indiana Univeristy. They become friends. Feburary 2014 they go on their first date.

After we first met:

After college the 2 briefly lived in Indianapolis together. As Megan finished her internship she got a full time job offer in Chicago and took it. Matt and Megan were long distance for about a year spending their weekends driving up or down I65 to see each other. Finally in Febuarary 2016 Matt moved to Chicago and the two were reunited. They moved in togher in January of 2018 and got engaged Feburary of 2020.
